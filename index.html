Number Guessing Game

Welcome to the Number Guessing Game! This simple web-based game challenges you to guess a secret number between 1 and 100. You have 10 attempts to guess the correct number.

How to Play

1. Enter your guess in the input field provided.
2. Click the "Guess" button to submit your guess.
3. You will receive hints such as "Try Higher" or "Try Lower" to guide you.
4. Keep guessing until you find the correct number or reach the maximum number of guesses.
5. If you guess correctly, you win the game!
6. If you reach the maximum attemts you lost.
